About

I began my career working as a digital retoucher for master photographer Mark Spencer of Carriage House Studios in Andover, MA. I learned the ins and outs of a world class portrait studio and became an expert in Adobe Photoshop. After working for Mark I went on to UMass Lowell to study design with a concentration in photography.

At school I worked with black and white film and developed prints in the darkroom. I also trained in more traditional arts like drawing, painting, and sculpture. School gave me a strong foundation in the creative process, allowing me to conceptualize a project and execute it to completion.

With my prior experience I gained employment at the area's leading camera retailer, Hunt's Photo, where I learned how to use professional digital camera equipment. I have since gone on to work as a freelance photo-journalist and started my own photography business.
